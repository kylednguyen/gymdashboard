# Gym Dashboard

My personal gym progress tracker — a private dashboard I built for myself to keep an
eye on my training and stay on top of my goals.

It pulls my own data from Airtable and shows it at a glance:

- **Workouts** — what I've trained and how often
- **Body metrics** — my weight trend over time
- **Goals** — progress toward the targets I'm chasing
- **Consistency** — my streak and how regular I've been

Just for me, on my phone.
